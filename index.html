Mix

Damion5050's tek is one of the most popular teks out there for growing mushrooms in bulk. It is simple, cheap and straight-forward method, often recommended to beginners for their first bulk grow.

Because the original Damion5050 tek is designed with simplicity as the main characteristic, there is some room for improvement:

  1. adding gypsum in mixture is good idea :)
  2. pasteurizing the substrate in pressure cooker before inoculation is recommended by some people, but it is not necessary
